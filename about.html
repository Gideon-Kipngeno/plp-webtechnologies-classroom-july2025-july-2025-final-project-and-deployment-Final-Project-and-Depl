About Greenwood High School

Founded in 1985, Greenwood High School has been a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development. Our mission is to nurture future leaders through rigorous education and a supportive community.

From humble beginnings, Greenwood has grown into one of the region’s most respected institutions. We pride ourselves on our legacy of innovation, inclusivity, and integrity.
