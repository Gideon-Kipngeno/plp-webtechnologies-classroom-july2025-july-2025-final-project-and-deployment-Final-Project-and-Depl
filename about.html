Welcome to Greenwood High

Greenwood High School has been shaping minds since 1985. Our mission is to empower students to lead with integrity, think critically, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our History

From a single classroom to a thriving campus, Greenwood High has grown through decades of innovation and community support. Our legacy is built on academic excellence and inclusive values.

Leadership & Faculty

Our leadership team and faculty are passionate educators committed to student growth. With diverse expertise and a shared vision, they foster a culture of excellence and care.

Our Journey

1985

Greenwood High founded with a single classroom and a bold vision for inclusive education.

1995

Expanded to include science labs, arts programs, and a full secondary curriculum.

2010

Launched our digital learning initiative and partnered with global education networks.

2025

Celebrating 40 years of excellence with new facilities and community outreach programs.
