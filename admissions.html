Apply to Greenwood High

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds. Here's everything you need to know to get started.

Application Steps

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Upload academic transcripts and ID
  3. Submit a recommendation letter
  4. Attend an interview and placement test
  5. Receive your admission decision
Start Application

Entry Requirements

  • Minimum GPA or KCPE/KCSE scores
  • Recommendation from a teacher or mentor
  • Proof of identity and age
  • Completed application form
