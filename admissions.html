Admissions

Join Greenwood High and become part of a vibrant learning community. We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds.

Application Process

  1. Submit online application
  2. Provide academic transcripts
  3. Attend interview and placement test
  4. Receive admission decision

Requirements

Applicants must provide proof of prior academic performance, a recommendation letter, and a completed application form.
